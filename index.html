HIPPO HEIST

How to Play

Your Mission

You're a hungry hippo helping feed a leopard! Steal bananas from trees and deliver them before time runs out.

Controls

WASD or Arrow Keys - Move

SPACE - Grab bananas / Feed leopard / Eat farmers

Tips & Tricks

  • Hide in the water canals to avoid farmers
  • Lure farmers near water, then EAT THEM for health!
  • You can carry up to 5 bananas at once
  • Feeding the leopard restores 1 health
  • Watch the timer - complete each level in 60 seconds!
